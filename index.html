Welcome to Puffin Island

Life Life the Puffin Way

Premium, Pitureesque Lodgings Very Close to Live Puffins.

Welcome to Puffin Island, Puffin Safaris for Puffin lovers and their friends who they trick into attending.
We offer a number of high-class lodgings next to active and thriving Puffin colonies. Puffin views are a Book-Now button click away. Day or night, winter or summer, Puffin Island offers the world's MOST ACCURATE experiential depiction of what your life would be like if you lived in a pigeon colony. Explore our listings below!

  • Plumage Place

    Welcome to Plumage Place. This premiere lodging sleeps three humans but is only 16 ft. from hundreds of puffins. A stay in this rustic, puffin-themed cabin will leave you saying "Wow, Puffins are much louder than I though," to you friends and family.

  • Puffin Peak

    Up, up and away. If you can make the poop-packed pilgrimidge to Puffin Peak, you'll be pleased by the abundance of aggressive male Puffins. Watch out! These little guys are very territorial. Lol. Puffin Peak sits atop Mt. LaMore, sleeps six humans and boast world-class views of these feathured fellows.

  • Burrow Bodega

    Bienvenido al Burrow Bodega. This lodging/corner store offers a costal view of Puffin nesting grounds and a wide selection of corner-store treats. This lodging sleep 12 humans, though, we should warn you. That would be pretty tight. Fun Fact: a burrow is a non-descript word for a group of Puffins. Welcome home.
